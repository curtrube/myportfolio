About Me

I'm a self-taught Web Developer with a background in Cloud and Network Engineering.

After Covid-19 struck in the U.S. I decided to spend my time in lockdown learning Full-Stack Web Development. I quickly found a passion for building cool web applications.

I started my career as a Network Engineer supporting medium-large enterprise networks. In 2016, I acheived the CCNA certification and continued to support and engineer data center networks until 2020. After 2019, I transitioned to be a Cloud Engineer with a focus on migrating applications and services to AWS.
